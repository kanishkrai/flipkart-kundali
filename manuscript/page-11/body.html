Future of E-commerce

As per India's chart, Rahu will transit in 3rd house of communication, so Indian startups will put efforts in establishing new platforms, improving UI/UX of existing platforms and try to keep in check with their sibling companies and their performances.

During Sep'2017 to Sep'2018, Jupiter will transit in 6th house of service and competition in the sign of Libra which signifies balance. Under the patronage of Libra, companies will compete by improving their customer service and maintaining a balance with the competitors. In Sep'2018 to Sep'2019, Jupiter will transit in 7th house of business/partnership in the sign of Scorpio which signifies mystery. This symbolises leads to startups coming forward and forming secret tieups/syndicates to fulfill hidden agendas.
