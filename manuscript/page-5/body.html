Synopsis

The largest E-Commerce company in India, Flipkart was founded in 2007 by Amazon drop-outs Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, both alumni of IIT-Delhi. It started with just selling books online. Today Flipkart has revolutionalized eCommerce market and sells everything from mobile phones to furniture. The company employs more than 20000 people. Flipkart is registered in Singapore, but operates out of Bangalore in India.
