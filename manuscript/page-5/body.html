Flipkart was founded in 2007 by ex-Amazon employees and alumni of IIT-Delhi Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. Today, it is the largest e-commerce company of India. They started with selling just books online but later on morphed into full e-commerce marketplace selling everything from mobile phones to furniture.

Flipkart is headquartered in Bangalore but is registered in Singapore. It employs over 20,000 people.
