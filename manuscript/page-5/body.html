Synopsis

The largest E-Commerce company in India, Flipkart was founded in 2007 by Amazon dropouts Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal both alumni of IIT-Delhi. It started with just selling books online, today Flipkart has revolutionalized Indian eCommerce market and sells everything from mobile phones to furniture. Flipkart is registered in Singapore and operates out of Bangalore, India employing over 20000 people.
